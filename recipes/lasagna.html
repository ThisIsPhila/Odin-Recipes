Delicious Classic Lasagna

Caution this is a made up recipe, intended for educational purposes!

Description

Lasagna is a classic dish that everyone loves. This lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce,
layered with lasagna noodles and cheese before baking until bubbly. This recipe will feed a big family
and freezes well too.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1 (8 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Italian salad dressing
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic. Mix well.
  3. Form the mixture into 1 1/2-inch meatballs.
  4. Place 1 1/2-inch meatballs into a 9-inch round baking dish.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
  7. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat.
  8. Add the lasagna and cook for 5 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes.
  10. Serve with any toppings you like.
