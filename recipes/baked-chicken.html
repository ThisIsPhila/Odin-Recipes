Easy Baked Chicken

Caution this is a made up recipe, intended for educational purposes!

Description

You can always turn to baked chicken for your weeknight dinner. Use a simple and basic recipe that is so easy
to make; you'll never get bored with it. This is one of the easiest, most flavorful baked chicken recipes ever.
It’s also a great recipe for beginners who might not know what to do with poultry that isn’t breaded and fried.
This easy to make chicken will be ready in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 to 4 pound) chicken,
  • 1 to 2 garlic cloves, minced, about 1 teaspoon (optional)
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup low sodium chicken stock or white wine for the gravy, optional

Directions

  1. Trim and salt the chicken
  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C)
  3. Prep the chicken
  4. Bake the chicken to 400°F (205°C)
  5. Let the chicken rest
  6. Make the gravy
  7. Serve with steamed rice, mashed potatoes, or Spanish rice
